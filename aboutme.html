About me

Some background..

I am from a small, picturesque and historic town in the western part of Greece called Nafpaktos. In 2015, I graduated high school and was admitted to the University of Patras(former University of Western Greece). After 4 and a half years, I completed my studies in 2020 and earned my bachelors.

After that, I decided not to follow my degree's field(Physiotherapy), and turned to programming instead. Web development quickly won me over, as I felt it's a field that allows for countless opportunities. Now, I'm studying some essential languages and frameworks, and I hope to become a full fledged developer, even if my academic background is not in a relevant field.

Some other things about you?

I'm (mostly) a laid back person, with a sense of responsibility. I love talking about technology, cars, ideas and the universe, while I'd never turn down a good story.

In my free time, I usually go out with friends, or I watch movies/TV shows. I'm also a gamer so chances are you'll find me with a headset on and a controller in my hands. Every now and then I do some lifting workouts, or I go for a run/cycling session.

Give me a bucket list!

Yes, like most people I have a list of things I'd like to do. Here they are:

  • Go skydiving.
  • Drive a really fast car, really fast.
  • Visit Bali and Maldives (and any tropical paradise).
  • About a thousand and six other things I can't fit here.
